Subscription FAQs

How do subscription payments work?

+

Subscriptions are paid monthly using PNGA tokens from your wallet. You can earn tokens by participating in the PangaFi ecosystem or purchase them directly.

Can I change my subscription anytime?

+

Yes! You can upgrade your subscription at any time. Downgrades will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What happens to my NFTs if I cancel?

+

Any NFTs you've minted remain in your wallet forever, even if you cancel your subscription. However, you'll lose the ability to mint new ones without an active subscription.
